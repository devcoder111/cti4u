Leveraging the Power of the Cloud By Offering Customers Workspace as a Service

Leading Unified Communications Helps SMBs Take Advantage of Emerging Technology

Communications Technologies Incorporated an industry leader in unified communications, announced today the release of their newest technology deployment, which is their WaaS (Workspace-as-a-service) program that is being offered to the SMBs (small to mid-sized businesses). The program is designed to help businesses make the proverbial jump to the cloud. As the number of executives increase, so does the demand for more flexible and secure applications, hardware, software and virtualized components. WaaS takes this a step further, by taking office technology to new heights by running every component through a virtualized network, instead of requiring local device management. Communications Technologies Incorporated is very excited to announce this program and to share the value-adds with their existing client base.
