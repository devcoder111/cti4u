Section 179 Tax Breaks

The Internal Revenue Code Section 179 provides a phenomenal way for companies to acquire new technology. Under Section 179 companies can take advantage of significant tax deductions thus having an immediate impact to the bottom line.

Section 179 was designed to give businesses the ability to increase spending on new equipment in order to stimulate the economy. Unfortunately, most companies are not aware of the tax advantages or the fact that the deduction may change in 2014.
