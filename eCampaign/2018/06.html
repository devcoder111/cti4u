CTI Bike Repair Party

CTI will be doing our annual bicycle repair and clean up on June 19th, 5pm till done. We receive bikes from area police departments and people who have them stored away.

We repair, refurbish and clean the bikes to be donated to the children at the Boys and Girls Club of Flint.

We will be providing food and beverage.

If you have a bike that you can donate or would like to help fix up the bikes on June 19th, please contact Jeff Allen (jallen@cti4u.com or 810-743-6900)
