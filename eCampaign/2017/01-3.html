CTI Educates Private Healthcare Practices on How to Handle HIPAA Audits

Informing Private Practice Owners on How to Thwart 6-Figure Penalty Fees for HIPAA Violations

CTI, a leading provider of unified communications, announced today that the company is educating private healthcare practices on how they can eliminate the risk of incurring large HIPAA penalties simply by updating some of their outdated technology. HIPAA compliance has long been thought of as an unenforceable regulation, with a distant threat of consequences, but in the past year, the government has added pressure to private practices like never before.

As a result, CTI is informing all of their current customers about these risks and how they can be eliminated altogether. CTI is also advising those customers on how to get their technology in order, and in compliance with HIPAA regulations before they are penalized with drastic fees, which can sometimes reach up to six figure penalties.
