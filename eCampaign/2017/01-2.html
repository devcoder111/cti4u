Important Phone System Security Notice

In recent months there have been several occurances of phone systems and voice mails getting hacked to allow call forwarding to oversea's locations. Bad people will then give this information out and the business ends up with long distance charges that could be in the thousands of dollars.

CTI strongly recommends that all mailboxes on your communications system have a unique password to prevent this from happening to your business. CTI is also available to perform a security audit and set up restrictions within the system to prevent this unwanted toll fraud.
