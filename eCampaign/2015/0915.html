Protect Yourself From a Virus

We have been alerted by some of our clients of a potentially dangerous (and sneaky) virus coming in through their email. All of the customers have reported the same, they receive an email with the subject line "Resume" that has an attachment. Upon opening the attachment, their computer is infected with the virus.

We would like to advise all of our customers to take caution when opening their emails (more so from email addresses you do not recognize). We also recommend that you have a corporate antivirus, which may prevent some of the issues and can also alert the you of a virus.

If you find that you have already been infected by this virus, please contact us so we can work to clean it off your network.

The silver lining of this situation is that all the customers affected were also taking advantage of our cloud backup solutions, so we were able to have them back up and running quickly.
